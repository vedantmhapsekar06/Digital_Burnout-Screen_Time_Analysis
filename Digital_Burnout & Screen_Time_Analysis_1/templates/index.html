Project Overview

Project: Digital Burnout & Screen Time Analytics

Dataset: 1,000 users · 10 features · Mobile usage records

Target: Engineered Burnout Risk Label (Low / Moderate / High / Extreme)

Split: 80% train / 20% test — Stratified sampling

Best Model: XGBoost — 87.5% accuracy, 0.981 ROC-AUC

Cities: New York, Phoenix, Chicago, Los Angeles, Houston

ML Pipeline Steps
01Data Collection & Import
02Preprocessing & Feature Engineering
03EDA — Distributions, Correlations
04Train-Test Split (80/20, stratified)
05Model Training — RF + XGBoost
06Evaluation — Accuracy, F1, ROC-AUC
Burnout Score Formula
Burnout_Score = (Daily_Screen_Time × 0.35) + (Social_Media × 0.30) + (Gaming × 0.20) + (Total_App_Usage × 0.15)
→ Normalized 0–100 → <25 Low | 25–50 Moderate | 50–75 High | >75 Extreme
